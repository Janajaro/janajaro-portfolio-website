↝ Tomorrowland █ █ █ █ █ █ █ Tomorrowland is a comic book autobiography for all the lazy souls out there. It takes place in the timeless haze of summer, surrounded by work obligations that never seem to end. Their relentless onslaught only fuels an ever-growing desire to escape, escape from reality, into a place we know, where we feel safe. But what happens to a mind that completely detaches from reality? How do our own ambitions harm us? And can you truly savor a cherry with the pit still inside?
Published both digitaly and as an risograph limited edition by Potato Publishing in Linz, Austria.
